Our Story

BORN FROM OBSESSION

Black Haven began with a single question: what does coffee taste like when nothing is compromised? No shortcuts, no blends built for volume — only intentional sourcing, honest relationships with farmers, and a relentless dedication to the craft.

We travel to the source, cup alongside farmers at altitude, and bring back only what moves us. Then we roast in small batches, by hand, to honour what the land gave.

Single Origin

WHERE IT BEGINS

We source directly from four extraordinary growing regions. Each cup carries the fingerprint of a specific farm, a specific season, a specific story.

🇪🇹
Ethiopia
Yirgacheffe · Guji

Floral and tea-like, with notes of jasmine, lemon zest, and stone fruit. The birthplace of coffee itself.

1,750 – 2,200 masl
🇰🇪
Kenya
Nyeri · Kirinyaga

Bold and bright. Blackcurrant, tomato acidity, and a deep, winey body that lingers.

1,600 – 2,000 masl
🇨🇴
Colombia
Huila · Nariño

Caramel sweetness, hazelnut, and a silky medium body. Reliable depth, endlessly satisfying.

1,500 – 1,900 masl
🇬🇹
Guatemala
Antigua · Huehuetenango

Dark chocolate, spice, and orange peel. Volcanic soil gives a complexity that demands attention.

1,400 – 1,800 masl
The Craft

FROM FARM TO CUP

01
Direct Sourcing

We visit farms, cup on-site, and build long-term relationships with producers. No middlemen, full transparency.

02
Small Batch Roasting

Each origin is roasted separately on our 12kg drum roaster. We profile meticulously, tasting every batch before it's approved.

03
Precision Brewing

Temperature-controlled extraction. Calibrated grind. Consistent tamp. Every variable mastered so you taste only the coffee.

04
Served Fresh

We roast to order and serve within the peak flavour window. Freshness is not optional at Black Haven — it's fundamental.
